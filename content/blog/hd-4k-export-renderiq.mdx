# HD & 4K Export With Renderiq

Choosing the right resolution depends on your use case. Here's when to use each.

## Resolution Options

### HD (1920x1080)

**Best for:**
- Web presentations
- Email attachments
- Social media
- Quick previews
- Online portfolios

**File size:** Smaller, faster to share
**Quality:** Excellent for digital use

### 4K (3840x2160)

**Best for:**
- Print materials
- Large displays
- High-quality presentations
- Professional portfolios
- Marketing materials

**File size:** Larger, higher quality
**Quality:** Print-ready, professional

## When to Use Each

### Use HD When

- Presenting on screens
- Sharing via email
- Posting online
- Quick client previews
- Testing concepts

### Use 4K When

- Printing materials
- Large format displays
- Final presentations
- Professional portfolios
- Marketing materials
