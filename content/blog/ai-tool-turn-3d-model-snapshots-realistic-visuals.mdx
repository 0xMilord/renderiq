## Why Rendering Feels Like a Chore

### Minutes to Learn, Hours to Render

If you’ve ever spent an entire afternoon elbow-deep in material settings, camera angles, and sunlight sliders, you’re not alone. Traditional rendering workflows go something like this:
- Gather and apply realistic materials from endless libraries.
- Adjust lights so your model isn’t a soulless gray block.
- Wrestle with cameras and render settings.
- Hit “render.” Wait. Make coffee. Re-render. Repeat.

**Result:** 2-8 hours or more, a few new gray hairs, and often, a still-unfinished look.

### The Technical Rabbit Hole

Render engines like V-Ray, Enscape, or Lumion pack amazing power… if you know what you’re doing:
- Steep learning curves
- Hardware that costs as much as a vacation
- Plugins on plugins 
- Never-ending updates and compatibility issues

### The Price Tag

Professional rendering software does not come cheap:
- V-Ray: $700+/year
- Enscape: $500+/year
- Lumion: $1,500+/year
- Add a powerful computer: $2,000+

Not exactly friendly for fast, everyday visualization.
