## Best Practices

### 1. Start with Clear Layouts

Well-defined floor plans produce better renders. Include:
- Store dimensions
- Fixture locations
- Entry/exit points
- Key zones (cash wrap, fitting rooms, etc.)

### 2. Describe Merchandising

Be specific about product placement:

```text
Good: "Products displayed on wall-mounted shelves 
with accent lighting, organized by category"

Bad: "Put stuff on shelves"
```

### 3. Use Reference Images

Upload brand guidelines or reference stores:

```text
Upload: Brand style guide
Prompt: "Apply this color palette and style" → @latest
Result: Brand-consistent visualization
```
