### Step 1: Prepare Your Sketch

Start with a clear sketch. The better your input, the better your output:

- **Clear linework**: Use dark, defined lines
- **Simple composition**: Focus on the main design elements
- **Good contrast**: Ensure your sketch has clear light/dark areas
- **Context clues**: Include elements that indicate scale (doors, windows, furniture)
