# Apps

24 specialized tools for quick, focused tasks. No prompts needed—just upload and go.

## Render Transformations

### [Render to Section Drawing](/apps/ai-architectural-section-drawing-tool)

Convert renders to technical CAD sections, 3D cross-sections, or illustrated 2D drawings with text control and style references for construction docs.

**What it does**: Transforms photorealistic renders into precise technical section drawings showing all structural elements, materials, and dimensions with architectural drafting standards.

**Use for**: Construction documentation, technical drawings, section views, permit applications

### [Render to CAD](/apps/convert-render-to-cad-drawing-tool)

Generate floor plans, elevations, and sections in batch with CAD linework, dimensions, and annotations for permit applications and construction docs.

**What it does**: Converts photorealistic renders into clean 2D CAD-style technical drawings with precise linework, dimensions, and architectural annotations.

**Use for**: Permit applications, construction documentation, technical drawings, CAD conversion

### [Render Upscale](/apps/ai-render-upscaler-architectural-enhancement)

Upscale renders 2x, 4x, or 8x with AI enhancement, sharpening edges, refining textures, and maintaining architectural accuracy for print-ready quality.

**What it does**: Increases render resolution while maintaining quality, detail, and architectural accuracy. Perfect for print-ready outputs.

**Use for**: Print-ready quality, high-resolution outputs, detail enhancement, large format printing

### [Render Effects](/apps/architectural-render-effects-stylization-tool)

Apply sketch, illustration, wireframe, watercolor, or pencil effects with adjustable intensity while preserving architectural proportions and design intent.

**What it does**: Adds creative effects to renders while maintaining architectural accuracy and design intent. Transform realistic renders into artistic styles.

**Use for**: Artistic presentations, style variations, creative effects, portfolio diversity

## How to Use Apps

1. Go to [Apps](/apps)
2. Browse or search for a tool
3. Click on the tool name or use the links above
4. Upload your image(s)
5. Configure settings (if needed)
6. Click **Generate**
7. Download or iterate

## Tips

**Choose the right tool**:
- Match tool to your task
- Read tool descriptions above
- Check input requirements (single image, multiple images, or image+text)

**Prepare your images**:
- Use clear, high-quality images
- Match tool requirements
- Check file formats (JPG, PNG, WebP)

**Iterate**:
- Try different settings
- Compare results
- Refine inputs
