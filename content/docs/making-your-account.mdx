# Making Your Account

Create your Renderiq account in minutes and start creating stunning architectural visualizations.

## Signing Up

### Option 1: Email and Password

1. Go to [Sign Up](/signup)
2. Enter your information:
   - Full name
   - Email address
   - Password (minimum 6 characters)
   - Confirm password
3. Accept the Terms of Service
4. Click "Create account"
5. Check your email for a verification link
6. Click the link to verify your email

**Important**: You need to verify your email before you can sign in.

### Option 2: Quick Signup with Google or GitHub

1. Go to [Sign Up](/signup)
2. Click "Google" or "GitHub"
3. Authorize Renderiq in the popup
4. Your account is created automatically

**Note**: Use the same provider (Google or GitHub) for future logins.

## Signing In

### Email and Password

1. Go to [Login](/login)
2. Enter your email and password
3. Check "Remember Me" to stay signed in (optional)
4. Click "Sign in"

**Having trouble?**
- Make sure you verified your email (check spam folder)
- Try resetting your password
- Contact support if issues persist

### Google or GitHub Login

1. Go to [Login](/login)
2. Click the same provider you used for signup
3. You're signed in automatically

## Understanding Credits

Renderiq uses credits to generate renders. You get **25 free credits** when you sign up.

### Credit Costs

**Image Generation**:
- Standard Quality: 5 credits
- High Quality: 10 credits
- Ultra Quality: 15 credits

**Video Generation**:
- Costs vary by duration and model

**Upscaling**:
- Varies based on scale factor

### Getting More Credits

1. **Free Credits**: 25 credits when you sign up
2. **Subscription Plans**: Monthly credits included
3. **One-time Purchase**: Buy credits from [Billing](/dashboard/billing)
4. **Referrals**: Coming soon

### Managing Credits

- View balance at [Billing](/dashboard/billing)
- Purchase credits anytime
- Track usage in transaction history

## Account Management

### Update Your Profile

1. Go to [Profile](/dashboard/profile)
2. Click "Edit Profile"
3. Update your information
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Change Password

1. Go to [Settings](/dashboard/settings)
2. Click "Change Password"
3. Enter current and new password
4. Click "Update Password"

### Email Verification

If you need to verify or change your email:

1. Go to [Settings](/dashboard/settings)
2. Click "Verify Email" or "Change Email"
3. Check your email for the verification link

### Delete Account

**Warning**: This is permanent and cannot be undone.

1. Go to [Settings](/dashboard/settings)
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone"
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Enter your password and type "DELETE" to confirm

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In

- Verify your email address is correct
- Check spam folder for verification email
- Use password reset if needed
- Clear browser cache
- Try incognito/private mode
- Contact support if issues persist

### Email Verification Issues

- Check spam/junk folder
- Wait a few minutes (emails can be delayed)
- Request a new verification email from settings
- Try a different email provider
- Contact support

### OAuth Login Issues

- Use the same provider (Google/GitHub) you used for signup
- Check OAuth account permissions
- Clear browser cache
- Try a different browser

### Credit Issues

- Refresh the page
- Wait a few minutes for sync
- Check transaction history
- Contact support with transaction details
