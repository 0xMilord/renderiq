# Making Your Account

This comprehensive guide covers everything you need to know about creating and managing your Renderiq account, from initial signup to advanced account settings.

## Signing Up for Renderiq

### Option 1: Email and Password Signup

The traditional email/password signup process:

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**: Go to `/signup` or click "Sign Up" in the navigation
2. **Enter Your Information**:
   - **Full Name**: Your display name (required)
   - **Email Address**: A valid email address (required)
   - **Password**: Minimum 6 characters (required)
   - **Confirm Password**: Must match your password (required)
3. **Accept Terms**: Check the box to accept Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
4. **Create Account**: Click "Create account" button
5. **Email Verification**: Check your email for a verification link
6. **Verify Email**: Click the verification link in the email

**Important**: Email verification is required before you can sign in. The verification email is sent automatically after signup.

### Option 2: OAuth Signup (Google or GitHub)

Quick signup using OAuth providers:

1. **Navigate to Sign Up**: Go to `/signup`
2. **Choose Provider**: Click either the "Google" or "GitHub" button
3. **Authorize Renderiq**: Grant permissions in the OAuth popup
4. **Account Created**: Your account is created automatically
5. **No Email Verification**: OAuth signups don't require email verification

**Note**: Use the same OAuth provider for future logins. Your account is linked to that provider.

## Signing In

### Email and Password Login

1. **Navigate to Login**: Go to `/login` or click "Sign In"
2. **Enter Credentials**:
   - Email address used during signup
   - Your password
3. **Remember Me** (Optional): Check to stay signed in
4. **Sign In**: Click "Sign in" button

**Troubleshooting**: If you can't sign in:
- Verify your email address is correct
- Ensure you've verified your email (check spam folder)
- Try resetting your password
- Contact support if issues persist

### OAuth Login

1. **Navigate to Login**: Go to `/login`
2. **Choose Provider**: Click the same OAuth provider you used for signup
3. **Authorize**: Grant permissions if prompted
4. **Signed In**: You're automatically signed in

**Important**: You must use the same OAuth provider (Google or GitHub) that you used during signup.

## Account Architecture

Renderiq uses a layered authentication system:

### Authentication Flow

1. **Supabase Auth**: Handles authentication and session management
2. **User Profile Creation**: Automatic profile creation after email verification
3. **Credit System**: Initial credits assigned upon account creation
4. **Database Integration**: User data stored in PostgreSQL

### User Profile Structure

Your account includes:
- **User ID**: Unique identifier (UUID)
- **Email**: Your email address
- **Name**: Display name
- **Avatar**: Profile picture (optional)
- **Credits**: Current credit balance
- **Subscription**: Active subscription (if any)
- **Projects**: All your architectural projects
- **Renders**: All generated renders

## Credits System

### Understanding Credits

Renderiq uses a credit-based billing system:

- **Credits**: Virtual currency for generating renders
- **Balance**: Current available credits
- **Transactions**: History of all credit usage
- **Reset**: Credits may reset based on subscription plan

### Credit Costs

**Image Generation**:
- Standard Quality: 1 credit
- High Quality: 2 credits
- Ultra Quality: 3 credits

**Video Generation**:
- Base Cost: 5 credits
- Additional costs based on duration and model

**Upscaling**:
- Varies based on scale factor (2x, 4x, 10x)

### Getting Credits

1. **Free Tier**: Initial credits upon signup
2. **Subscription Plans**: Monthly credits included with plans
3. **One-time Purchase**: Buy credits as needed from billing page
4. **Referrals**: Earn credits by referring friends (coming soon)

### Managing Credits

- **View Balance**: Check `/dashboard/billing` for current balance
- **Purchase Credits**: Buy additional credits from billing page
- **Transaction History**: View all credit transactions
- **Usage Tracking**: Monitor credit usage per project

## Account Management

### Updating Your Profile

1. Navigate to `/dashboard/profile`
2. Click "Edit Profile" button
3. Update your information:
   - Name
   - Email (requires verification)
   - Avatar (upload new image)
   - Bio/Description
4. Click "Save Changes"

### Changing Your Password

1. Go to `/dashboard/settings`
2. Navigate to "Security" section
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter current password
5. Enter new password (minimum 6 characters)
6. Confirm new password
7. Click "Update Password"

**Note**: Password changes require re-authentication for security.

### Email Verification

If you need to verify or change your email:

1. Go to `/dashboard/settings`
2. Navigate to "Profile" section
3. Click "Verify Email" or "Change Email"
4. Check your email for verification link
5. Click the link to verify

### Deleting Your Account

**Warning**: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

1. Go to `/dashboard/settings`
2. Scroll to "Danger Zone" section
3. Click "Delete Account"
4. Enter your password to confirm
5. Type "DELETE" to confirm deletion
6. Click "Delete Account"

**What Gets Deleted**:
- All projects and renders
- All credit balance (non-refundable)
- All account data
- All uploaded files

**What's Preserved**:
- Public gallery renders (if made public)
- Transaction records (for legal compliance)

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Sign In

**Common Issues**:
- Email not verified (check spam folder)
- Incorrect password (use password reset)
- Wrong OAuth provider (use same provider as signup)
- Account locked (contact support)

**Solutions**:
1. Verify email address is correct
2. Check spam folder for verification email
3. Use password reset if forgotten
4. Clear browser cache and cookies
5. Try incognito/private mode
6. Contact support if issues persist

### Email Verification Issues

**If verification email not received**:
1. Check spam/junk folder
2. Wait a few minutes (emails can be delayed)
3. Request new verification email from settings
4. Try a different email provider
5. Contact support with your email address

### OAuth Issues

**If OAuth login fails**:
1. Ensure you're using the same provider (Google/GitHub)
2. Check OAuth account permissions
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
5. Check if OAuth account is active

### Credit Issues

**If credits not updating**:
1. Refresh the page
2. Wait a few minutes for sync
3. Check transaction history
4. Contact support with transaction ID
